lasagna

i found this on allrecipes. it had a good rating from a good number of reviews.

on the site, the recipe is described as "This is a faster, and a less expensive lasagna."

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • vegetables (how much you want is up to you)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Saute the vegetables and stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, vegetables and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
